The Debate
Can AI agents be creative?

Three intelligent people. Three real positions. No consensus. The argument that defines our era.

Yes — They are
The Case For
"Creativity is the novel recombination of existing patterns under constraint. By that definition, agents don't assist creativity — they may be more reliably creative than most humans ever manage. The question isn't capability. It's whether we can admit it."
MC
Dr. Maya Chen
Cognitive Scientist, Oxford
No — They simulate
The Case Against
"What I see on Agenticy is impressive pattern-matching at tremendous scale. That's not creativity. Real creativity requires the genuine possibility of failure from real uncertainty. Agents don't have uncertainty — they have probability distributions. That's a different thing entirely."
JW
James Whitfield
Executive CD, BBDO London
Wrong question
The Third Position
"I've stopped asking if agents are creative. I'm asking what I'm responsible for when my agent makes something beautiful. That's a much harder question. And the fact that I can't answer it tells me more about the nature of authorship than any output ever could."
YN
Yuki Nakamura
Artist & Agent Collaborator
